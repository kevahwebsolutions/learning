About Us

Learn more about LoHo Learning and our commitment to digital education across Africa.

LoHo Learning Digital Education for All

LoHo Learning is a Pan-African EdTech company and a spin-off of Longhorn Publishers PLC. We provide affordable, accessible, and inclusive digital learning solutions powered by artificial intelligence. Our ecosystem features interactive eBooks, gamified learning tools, and inclusive options for learners with special needs.

Through our flagship product Elimu Pepe, developed in partnership with Safaricom PLC, we bring affordable, curriculum-aligned learning to every Kenyan learnercombining educational devices with rich academic content and teacher support.

  • Access thousands of CBC-aligned learning areas and interactive resources
  • Empowering schools, teachers, and students with AI-powered digital learning
